Creating a New Persistent Image

Persistent images may be created directly through the Persistent Images page. You can also use the Schedules page to schedule future or recurring persistent images.

To create a new persistent image

  1. On the primary navigation bar, choose Disks.
  2. Choose Persistent Storage Manager.
  3. Choose Persistent Images.
  4. In the Tasks list, choose New.
  5. In the Volumes to include list, choose the volumes you want to include in the image.
  6. Select the Read-only or Read/Write button.
  7. Select a retention weight from the Retention weight list.
  8. Type your image's name in the Image name box.
  9. Choose OK.
