Deleting a Persistent Image

To delete a persistent image

  1. On the primary navigation bar, choose Disks.
  2. Choose Persistent Storage Manager.
  3. Choose Persistent Images.
  4. Select the persistent image you want to delete.
  5. In the Tasks list, choose Delete.
  6. Choose OK.
