Viewing UPS Status

From this page, you can display the UPS status and view configuration parameters.

To view the status of the UPS connected to the server appliance

  1. On the primary navigation bar, choose Status.
  2. Select UPS from the Status area.

          The UPS status information is displayed.


The following table details the UPS status values.

Value Description Example
Current power source The source that the UPS uses to provide power to your system, either AC power or UPS battery power. AC power
Estimated UPS run time The approximate number of minutes the UPS can support your system on battery power. 56 minutes
Estimated UPS capacity The present battery capacity of your UPS as a percentage of its maximum capacity. 100%
Battery condition The condition of your UPS battery. Good
Manufacturer The name of the company that manufactured your UPS. American Power Conversion
Model The model name of your UPS. Smart-UPS
Serial number The serial number of your UPS. NS9952140407
Firmware revision The firmware revision of your UPS. 50.11.D
